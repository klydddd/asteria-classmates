The Problem

180+ PHILIPPINE LANGUAGES. NO DATA. NO TOOLS.

The Data Gap

Fewer than 5 local languages have usable, clean speech data. Voice interfaces and accessibility tech simply do not function for the vast majority of Filipinos.

The Tooling Gap

Zero general-use open tools exist for cleaning or processing regional Philippine audio data. Researchers duplicate effort, spending 90% of their time writing custom scripts.

"Most Philippine languages are invisible to speech technology because researchers lack the basic infrastructure to even begin."
